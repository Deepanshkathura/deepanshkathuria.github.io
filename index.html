About us 

Mahadev Tyre House was founded in 1989 and is a well-known tyre dealer in Gurgaon. For over 30 years Mahadev Tyre House has been offering branded tyres to the motorists in Gurgaon and neighboring areas. We have a wide selection of tyres from major brands like Michelin and others including all-season, performance, all-terrain tyres etc. Besides tyre replacement and tyre repairs we also offer car services. Some of the tyres and car services that we offer include wheel alignment, wheel balancing, tyre rotation, nitrogen tyre inflation etc.

Services

new tyres

Having a new set of tires will provide a cushion of safety for the next 65,000 to 100,000 miles of the vehicle's life (depending on the tire tread-wear rating)

Tyre Fitting

Specifically, tyre fitters work with cars to fit tyres onto vehicles. They also repair tyres as needed.

Nitrogen Inflation

Nitrogen inflated tyres exhibit less pressure change with temperature swings. So if you are into Motorsports, it will make quite some difference as the tyres heat up during race.

Dampers

A shock absorber or damper is a mechanical or hydraulic device designed to absorb and damp shock impulses. It does this by converting the kinetic energy of the shock into another form of energy (typically heat) which is then dissipated.

alignment

Wheel alignment is part of standard automobile maintenance that consists of adjusting the angles of wheels. The purpose of these adjustments is to reduce tire wear 

car sanitization

Sanitizing the vehicle means your vehicle will be cleaned and disinfected from any kind of germs and viruses and will make sure that it won't get affected sometime soon.
